Finding Us

1 Mill Street is located in the heart of Leamington Spa, a vibrant town known for its Regency architecture and leafy parks. Below are directions to help you reach us, whether you're driving, taking the train, or using public transport.

By Train

Leamington Spa has excellent rail connections to London, Birmingham, and the surrounding areas. Leamington Spa Train Station (CV31 3NS) is approximately a 10-minute walk from 1 Mill Street. The station is served by regular trains from major cities, including:

  • London Marylebone: Approx. 1 hour 15 minutes.
  • Birmingham Moor Street: Approx. 30 minutes.
  • Oxford: Approx. 50 minutes.

By Bus

Leamington Spa's local bus services provide excellent connectivity, with several routes passing near Mill Street:

  • Route 67/67A: Stops near Clemens Street, a 3-minute walk from 1 Mill Street.
  • Route U1 (Uni Express): Stops at Lower Avenue, close to Leamington Spa Station, with a short walk to Mill Street.

By Car

If you're driving, 1 Mill Street is conveniently located near several public car parks:

  • St. Peter's Multi-Storey Car Park (CV32 5EL): Just a 5-minute walk from 1 Mill Street, this car park offers plenty of spaces, including disabled parking. Open 24 hours.
